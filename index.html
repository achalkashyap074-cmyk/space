Beyond Our Solar System

Stars & Constellations

The closest star system to Earth is Alpha Centauri, 4.37 light-years away. Our night sky contains 88 officially recognised constellations with billions of stars.

The Milky Way

Our home galaxy is a barred spiral spanning 100,000 light-years across, containing an estimated 100–400 billion stars and at least as many planets.

The Multiverse

Theoretical physics suggests our universe may be one of infinite universes comprising a multiverse, each governed by different physical laws and constants.
